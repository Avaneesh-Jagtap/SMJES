Welcome to SMJES

SHRI MAHAVIR JAIN ENGLISH SCHOOL is run by Jain Charitable Trust (Regd.) Kalyan. It is recognised by the Government of Maharashtra. The school comprises of Jr.kg. to X standard and the medium of instruction is English. The school aims at achieving excellence in every aspect of Educational, Spiritual, Academic, Cultural, Social & Physical activities, thus assuring total development of human personality. Our chief aim in educating the youth is to build proper interest, attitude and sense of values in them. Thus we hope to mould them into persons of character.

ABOUT US

Shri Mahavir Jain English School is an English medium school. It is run by the Jain Charitable Trust (Regd), Kalyan. It is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra. The school comprises of standards from Jr. KG to X. with more than 2000 students.

The school gives all round education to the children. No discrimination is made on the basis of caste, creed, or religion. Following Lord’s Mahavir’s message of “AHINSA”, the management of the school works to give abundance of love and care to the children through knowledge and selfless service.

SCHOOL AIM :

SHRI MAHAVIR JAIN ENGLISH SCHOOL with the aim of providing quality education, knowledge of Indian culture & Lord Mahavir’s message of ‘AHINSA’.

Objectives:
